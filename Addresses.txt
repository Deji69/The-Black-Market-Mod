Possibly useful addresses for druggies:

0x553F00 -> Render textures / Render lights (?) If disabled, player/actors are completely black

0x5533B0 -> Something with light

0x754B26 : jnz short LightingLoop (NOP this to produce rainbow effects) default 117.

0x754AEF : jle short Retn (Write 0xEB to make this a jump for no lighting at all) 126
